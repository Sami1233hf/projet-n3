Basic Rules

  • Each team has 11 players including a goalkeeper.
  • The match lasts 90 minutes, divided into two halves.
  • The team with most goals wins.
  • No handball except for the goalkeeper.
  • Offside rule applies when a player is ahead of the last defender.
